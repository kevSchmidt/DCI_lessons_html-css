Nemo is a young clownfish!
He lives with his father Marlin in the Great Barrier Reef. Nemo, despite being hampered by a lame fin, is eager to explore life around the ocean.

Along the way, Marlin learns to take risks and comes to terms with Nemo taking care of himself.
